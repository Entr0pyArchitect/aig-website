# American Innovations Group

American Innovations Group is a technology, engineering, IT, and cybersecurity company focused on practical solutions for real-world technical problems.

We support customers who need help planning, building, reviewing, or improving technology systems across software, hardware-adjacent engineering, industrial equipment concepts, and cybersecurity services.

## What We Do

American Innovations Group provides quote-based technology products and services across several lanes:

- **Cybersecurity solutions** - security audits, penetration testing scoping, network and Wi-Fi security reviews, GitHub/security hygiene support, incident-readiness planning, and cybersecurity tutoring.
- **Software solutions** - custom software planning, prototype web applications, automation tools, dashboards, workflow support, and technical documentation.
- **Hardware and engineering support** - product planning, embedded-systems planning, industrial equipment concepts, controller and motor-related planning, and quote-based technical consultation.
- **Consultation and project direction** - customer-defined technical scoping, architecture review, roadmap planning, and practical implementation guidance.

## Product and Quote Model

Most offerings are quote-based because customer requirements vary by scope, risk, timeline, environment, and technical complexity. The website allows customers to review product lanes, request quotes, and start a secure checkout or manual validation process after an amount has been approved.

## Payment Options

American Innovations Group supports several payment paths depending on the customer and quote type:

- PayPal hosted checkout
- Credit/debit card options handled through PayPal-supported secure checkout
- Bitcoin invoice workflow with transaction-hash submission and manual validation
- Cash App manual ticket workflow with manual validation

American Innovations Group does not collect raw card numbers directly on this website.

## Customer Safety

Customers should never send passwords, private keys, wallet seed phrases, bank credentials, confidential company credentials, or sensitive personal information through public forms. If a project requires sensitive review, American Innovations Group will coordinate a safer intake process.

## Company Direction

American Innovations Group is built around practical engineering, customer-defined requirements, responsible technology use, and professional delivery. The goal is to provide clear, useful, and ethically scoped technical solutions without overpromising or exposing customers to unnecessary risk.
